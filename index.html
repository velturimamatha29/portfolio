Professional Summary

What I actually build

I'm a Senior Data & AI Engineer with 10+ years of experience designing and deploying production-grade data and AI solutions across regulated, high-stakes industries — auto lending, pharmaceutical research, public transit, and insurance. My work spans two connected layers: the data engineering foundation (ingestion, transformation, warehousing, governance) that every model depends on, and the ML/GenAI layer built on top of it — RAG architectures, multi-agent systems, and continuously retrained production models that automate decision workflows while meeting compliance requirements like HIPAA, GxP, and FDA 21 CFR Part 11.

Recent work includes production-grade RAG pipelines on Amazon Bedrock, autonomous multi-agent systems using LangChain and LangGraph, and continuous retraining pipelines for large quantitative risk models — cutting manual analysis time by up to 45%. I care about the full lifecycle: from data exploration and feature engineering through CI/CD-enabled production deployment, monitoring, and governance.
